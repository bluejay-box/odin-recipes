Earl Grey Milk Tea

Description

An earl grey latte, or milk tea. Good to drink while you're working, or trying to not think about working.

Ingredience

  • Your preferred choice of milk
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Loose Earl Grey tea, around 1.5 tsp

Steps

  1. Boil water until around 200 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Add tea leaves and stir. Wait one minute for tea to steep.
  3. Add sugar and let dissolve.
  4. Add milk. Add ice cubes if preferred.
