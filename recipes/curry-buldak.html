Curry Buldak

Description

This recipe is a simple, easy to make dish that can be done in under twenty minutes. It's flavorful, colorful, and can be nutrient dense depending on what you have in the fridge. A perfect dish for when you're busy, gloomy, or just craving something spicy and chock full of flavor to get your through your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Buldak Ramen x2 Spicy
  • 1 cube Golden Curry Instant Curry (powdered form also viable)
  • 1/4 head napa cabbage
  • Fish cakes to taste
  • 1 egg
  • Mushrooms to taste
  • Firm tofu to taste

Steps

  1. Boil about three cups of water, adjust as needed
  2. Add sauce packet to water
  3. Add cabbage, mushrooms, and egg and let cook until cabbage is soft
  4. Add curry cube and let dissolve
  5. Add instant noodles
  6. Once the noodles are about firm, add fish cakes and firm tofu
  7. Cook for a few more minutes and then serve. Adjust cooking times depending on personal tastes but make sure the egg is cooked.
