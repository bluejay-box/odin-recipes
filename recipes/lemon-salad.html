Lemon Salad

Description

This is just some lemon salad. Make it in ten minutes and eat it. Make sure the lemon isn't moldy though

Ingredients

    Add everything to taste

  • Lemon
  • Olive oil
  • Baby spinach
  • Baby arugula
  • Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • Parmesan cheese, grated or shaved it doesn't matter do what you like

Steps

  1. Add veg to a bowl
  2. Mix lemon juice and olive oil (oh yeah juice the lemon first btw) and mix until cloudy
  3. Add lemon olive oil vinaigrette to the bowl and stir until the veg is all coated
  4. Add parmesan cheese to taste
